What is RSS?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) is a way to receive automatic updates from TrueNews. By subscribing to our RSS feeds, you can get the latest news delivered directly to your RSS reader or news aggregator.

How to Use RSS Feeds

  2. Copy the RSS feed URL for your desired category
  3. Paste the URL into your RSS reader
  4. Start receiving automatic updates
