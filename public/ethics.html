Our Commitment to Ethical Journalism

At TrueNews, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of journalistic integrity and ethical conduct. Our Code of Ethics serves as a guide for all our journalists, editors, and staff members in their pursuit of truth and accuracy.

Accuracy and Truth

  • We verify all information before publication
  • We correct errors promptly and transparently
  • We distinguish between news and opinion
  • We use reliable sources and fact-checking methods

Independence and Impartiality

  • We maintain editorial independence
  • We avoid conflicts of interest
  • We present diverse viewpoints
  • We separate news from advertising

Fairness and Respect

  • We treat all subjects with respect
  • We give subjects opportunity to respond
  • We protect vulnerable individuals
  • We respect privacy rights

Transparency

  • We disclose our sources when possible
  • We explain our editorial decisions
  • We acknowledge our mistakes
  • We maintain clear separation between news and sponsored content

Accountability

  • We take responsibility for our content
  • We respond to reader concerns
  • We maintain high professional standards
  • We regularly review and update our ethical guidelines
