Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Subscription

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign In" button in the top right corner and select "Create Account" to register for a new account.

How do I manage my subscription?

Log in to your account and go to "Account Settings" to manage your subscription preferences.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

Content & Features

How do I save articles for later?

Click the bookmark icon on any article to save it to your reading list.

How do I share articles?

Use the share buttons at the top of each article to share via social media or email.

How do I customize my news feed?

Go to "Preferences" in your account settings to customize your news feed and notifications.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

TrueNews supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

How do I report a technical issue?

Use the "Report Issue" button at the bottom of the page or contact our support team.

How do I clear my cache and cookies?

Use the "Delete All Cookies" button in the footer or clear your browser cache through your browser settings.
